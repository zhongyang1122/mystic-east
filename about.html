About Mystic East

Mystic East translates BaZi into plain English for people who already enjoy astrology, tarot, MBTI, and symbolic ways of understanding themselves.

東

Who made this?

Mystic East is a small independent product, not a large astrology company. The goal is simple: keep the free chart private, make the language easy to understand, and help Western readers experience BaZi without needing to learn classical Chinese first.

The site is built as a static web experience. The calculator runs in your browser. Birth details are not sent to a server by the free chart tool.

What this is and is not

This is a self-discovery tool. It can help you name patterns, ask better questions, and reflect on career, relationships, energy, and timing.

It is not medical, financial, legal, or mental health advice. It is also not a promise that your life is fixed by a birth chart.
